How to Acquire

Follow the path to own a piece of the origin.

1

Create a Wallet

Download a crypto wallet like MetaMask or Trust Wallet from the app store or as a browser extension. Create your wallet and secure your seed phrase.

2

Get Some ETH

You'll need ETH in your wallet to swap for $BBC. You can buy ETH directly in your wallet or transfer it from an exchange like Coinbase or Binance.

3

Swap for $BBC

Go to a decentralized exchange like Uniswap. Connect your wallet, paste the $BBC contract address, and swap your ETH for the bear.
